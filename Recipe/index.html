Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

  • one 9_inch pie
  • 30 Min - Prep time
  • 10 Min - Cook time
  • 40 Min - Total
  • Serving

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp constrach
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 eggs yolks, beaten
  • 1(9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350 degree F(175 degree C).
  2. Make Lemon Filling: In a medium saucepan....
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar, flour, constarch, and salt
    • Stir in water, lemon juice and lemon zest
    • Cook over medium high heat, stirring frequently, until mixture comes to a boil.
    • Stir in butter
    • Place egg yollks in a small gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture
    • Whisk egg yollk mixtures back into remaining sugar mixture.
    • Bring to boil and continue to cook while stirring constantly until thick
    • Remove from heat.
    • Pour filling into baked pastry shell.
  3. Make Mergingue: in a large glass or metal bowl..
    • whip egg whites until foamy.
    • Add sugar gradually, and continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
    • Spread meringue over pie, sealing the edges at the crust
  4. Bake: Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until meringue is golden brown.
